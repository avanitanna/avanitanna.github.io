Currently, I am building software to operate AI at scale at [Domino.ai (Domino Data Lab)](https://domino.ai/) where I get to combine my interests in Software Engineering and Machine Learning and create impact in this age of AI!  

I hold a Master's degree in Computer Science from [UC Santa Barbara](https://www.ucsb.edu/), where I focused on applications and systems and was fortunate to be advised by [Prof. William Wang](https://sites.cs.ucsb.edu/~william/) and [Prof. Amr El Abbadi](https://sites.cs.ucsb.edu/~amr/). I also hold a Bachelor’s and Master’s degree in Electrical Engineering from [IIT Bombay](https://www.iitb.ac.in/), where I worked on Auction Theory and Game Theory and was fortunate to be advised by [Prof. D Manjunath](https://iitb.irins.org/profile/11188). I have previously worked as a data scientist at the [International Committee of the Red Cross](https://www.icrc.org/en) in Geneva, as a data analytics consultant at [Carlson Wagonlit Travel](https://www.mycwt.com/) in Paris, and as a researcher at [TU Munich](https://www.tum.de/en/), [Columbia Business School](https://business.columbia.edu/) in New York and the [Weizmann Institute of Science](https://www.weizmann.ac.il/pages/) in Israel. I am grateful for these diverse, international experiences that have shaped my personality - they allow me to bring a unique perspective to the table! 

When I am not working, I love traveling, reading books, doing sports, dancing, spending time with loved ones, and painting (occasionally)! I am a generally curious person interested in learning  new things - feel free to contact me for a chat!
